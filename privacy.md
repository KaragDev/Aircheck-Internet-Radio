Privacy Policy
The Aircheck Internet Radio app does not collect personal, private, or identifying information.
- Saved information (favorite radio stations) stays resident on the users device.
- 3rd party APIs are utilized for station searching and content metadata enrichment - see the individual services for their privacy policies and practices:
  - [Radio Station Search API](https://radio-browser.info)
  - [iTunes Search API](https://performance-partners.apple.com/search-api)
